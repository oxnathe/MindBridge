Managing Anxiety

  • Practice the 5-4-3-2-1 grounding technique when feeling overwhelmed
  • Create a worry time - dedicate 15 minutes daily to process anxious thoughts
  • Keep a list of activities that calm you for quick reference
  • Challenge negative thoughts by asking "Is this thought based on facts?"

Improving Sleep Quality

  • Maintain a consistent sleep schedule, even on weekends
  • Create a relaxing bedtime routine starting 30 minutes before sleep
  • Limit screen time at least 1 hour before bed
  • Keep your bedroom cool, dark, and quiet for optimal sleep
